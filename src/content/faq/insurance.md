---
question: "Do you take insurance?"
order: 2
---

I don't take insurance directly. That said, you may have partial out-of-network coverage through your insurance provider. I can give you an invoice to submit for any reimbursement you're eligible for.

If you'd like to know your coverage before we begin, call your insurance provider and ask about the details of your out-of-network coverage for therapy.

You're also likely able to pay for sessions with a Health Savings Account (HSA).
